Balance Ledger
Track inter-store credit — goods exchanged with nearby shops without immediate payment
⚖️ How it works:
↑ I Took Goods — Your shop took goods from a nearby shop in emergency. Your stock increases temporarily. Your payable balance goes up until returned/paid.
↓ I Gave Goods — A nearby shop took goods from you. Your stock decreases temporarily. Your receivable balance goes up until returned/paid.
Click Settle once goods are returned or payment is made.
