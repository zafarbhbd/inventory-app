Balance Ledger
Track inter-store credit — goods exchanged without immediate payment
⚖️ How Balance Ledger works:
↑ I Took (I Owe) — Your store took goods from a nearby shop in emergency. They owe stock back or you pay later. Adds to your payable balance.
↓ I Gave (They Owe) — Another shop needed goods from you. They will return or pay later. Adds to your receivable balance.
Mark as Settled once returned/paid. Only pending entries affect Net Balance & stock.

Cash Book
Track all cash coming in and going out of your shop
💵 How Cash Book works:
Cash In — Money received: sales payments, ledger settlements received, any other income.
Cash Out — Money paid: purchase payments, ledger settlements paid, expenses, rent etc.
Cash on Hand = Total Cash In − Total Cash Out. This is your actual physical cash balance.
