Balance Ledger
Track inter-store credit — goods taken or given without immediate payment
⚖️ How Balance Ledger works:
↑ I Owe Them — Your store took goods from another store without paying yet. This increases your payable balance.
↓ They Owe Me — Another store took goods from you without paying yet. This increases your receivable balance.
Mark entries as Settled once payment is made. Only pending entries affect your Net Balance.
